Gianluca Della Vedova

Gianluca Della Vedova is Associate Professor of Computer Science at the Univ. Milano-Bicocca where he heads the research lab on Experimental Algorithmics.
His research focuses on combinatorial algorithms in Bioinformatics, and especially on sequence alignment, phylogeny inference, haplotyping, and alternative splicing detection. He has also worked on graph decomposition, clustering, and data privacy. He has a PhD in Computer Science from Univ. of Milano. He has supervised 3 PhD students as well as several undergraduate students. He has taught courses dedicated to students majoring in Computer Science, Statistics, Bioinformatics, and Data Science.
