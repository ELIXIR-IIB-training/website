Marco Moretto

Marco Moretto obtained a Master degree in Bioinformatics at the University of Trento working on biological meta-data integration through Semantic-Web technologies and graph-based approaches. He continued with a PhD at the University of Padua in Computational Biology developing tools for on transcriptomic data analysis and data integration. He works at the Research and Innovation Centre of Fondazione Edmund Mach starting from 2007 and has been involved in several projects from genomics to transcriptomic data analysis
