Patricia Palagi

I'm a biologist and computer scientist by training. My research interests were on neural networks, proteomics, and bioinformatics for mass spectrometry analysis. Since 1999, almost as long as I have been in the SIB, I have been teaching bioinformatics for proteomics and programming languages, and organising a variety of bioinformatics education and training workshops, courses and events for a wide audience. Today, I lead the SIB Training group and co-lead the ELIXIR Training Platform, and I'm very interested in best practices for training new trainers (train the trainers).
