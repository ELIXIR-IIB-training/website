Vincenza Colonna

Vincenza Colonna is group leader at the Institute of Genetics and Biophysics of the Italian National Research Council in Napoli, Italy. She works in human genomics and her main research interests are natural selection and consanguineous populations. She graduated from University of Napoli Federico II (Italy) and did postdoctoral work at University of Ferrara (Italy) and at the Wellcome Trust Sanger Institute (UK). She was lectures in Genetics and Bioinformatics at the University of Ferrara (Italy). She is also leading the ObiLab project (http://www.igb.cnr.it/popgenlab/research) on training in bioinformatics.
