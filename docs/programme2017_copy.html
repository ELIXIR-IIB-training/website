  • 2nd Regional Technical Workshop - Italy
    National Funding: build awareness on policy perspectives.
    The second InRoad Regional Workshop was held on the 27 and 28 November 2017 and took place at the National Research Council (CNR) of Italy in Rome. The purpose of the workshop was to discuss policy aspects linking Research Infrastructure roadmapping with national funding as well as with other funding instruments. The aim was to build awareness across the different types of stakeholders such as RI managers, funders and policy-makers from different countries. Roma, IT, 27-28 Nov 2017
    Participant: Loredana Le Pera
